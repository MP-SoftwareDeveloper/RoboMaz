Connect Your Phone to Qt Creator
Step 1 — Enable Developer Mode on your phone

Go to Settings → About Phone
Tap "MIUI Version" 7 times rapidly
You'll see "You are now a developer"

Step 2 — Enable USB Debugging

Go to Settings → Additional Settings → Developer Options
Enable "USB Debugging"
Also enable "Install via USB"

Step 3 — Connect via USB

Plug phone into PC with a USB cable
On your phone, tap "Allow" when the USB debugging prompt appears
Select "File Transfer (MTP)" mode for the USB connection

Step 4 — Qt Creator
Your phone should now appear in the bottom-left device selector instead of the emulator.
